Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy is based on Automattic. This privacy policy relates to every Blackberry and Android app developed by Amelin , except those which specifically name other policy.

Blackberry and Android App Users

Like most website and app operators, Amelin collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain users of Amelin's apps choose to interact with the app in ways that require Amelin to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Amelin gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask users who sign up for an account (in apps that require so) within the Amelin mobile applications to provide an email address. In each case, Amelin collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the user’s interaction with the app. Amelin does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And users can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain app-related activities.

Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, Amelin may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Amelin’s sole discretion. Amelin encourages users to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
